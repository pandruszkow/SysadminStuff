IntelliJ keymap overrides:

Redo: Ctrl-Y
Back: Alt-left
Close: Ctrl-F4, Ctrl-W
Delete line: Ctrl-K
Editor Select Word (broaden selection): Ctrl-*
Editor UnSelect Word (narrow down selection): Ctrl-Shift-*
Forward: Alt-right
Full screen: F11
Quick JavaDoc preview: F1
Toggle Distraction-free full screen (macro): Shift-F11
